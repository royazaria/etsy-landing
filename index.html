Frequently Asked Questions

How does EtsyPro AI actually work?

Our system uses your product photo and category information to generate a complete Etsy listing. We leverage data from EtsyHunt and our personal experience running 10+ Etsy shops with over $300,000 in sales. Using EtsyHunt's pointing system, we know exactly which optimization strategies are effective and which aren't. This real-world expertise is built into our AI to create tailored, high-converting listings for your products.

Is this compliant with Etsy's terms of service?

Absolutely. Our AI creates 100% unique content for each listing, ensuring no duplication even for identical products. The system is specifically designed to maintain compliance with Etsy's requirements for original content. You maintain full ownership of all generated content for your listings.

What happens to my original product photos?

Your original product photos are used as the source material for our enhanced images, but they're never shared with other users. We keep your original product intact during the enhancement process - only changing backgrounds, lighting, and presentation elements. Your product's color, texture, and form remain exactly the same.

Early access members will receive priority invitations, exclusive bonus credits, and discounted rates. Joining the waitlist ensures you'll be among the first to use the complete technology.

How much time will this actually save me?

For Etsy sellers who upload a new product daily (which is typical), you're spending about 7-14 hours per week on listing creation. EtsyPro AI reduces this to just 15-35 minutes weekly for the same number of listings. That's up to 50+ hours saved per month that you can reinvest in growing your business or enjoying your life!

Can I edit the content after it's generated?

Yes, you have full control to edit any part of the generated content. While most users find the AI results ready to use immediately, you can always make adjustments to match your brand voice or add specific product details that you want to emphasize.

What does a "token" actually get me?

We use a token system where each token equals one USD. In the complete EtsyPro (beyond the beta), you'll be able to choose exactly what you need generated or modified, similar to building a sandwich at Subway. The system will charge tokens based on the complexity of your request and the category of features you select. This gives you complete flexibility to pay only for what you actually need.

How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
