EtsyPro AI Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2025

1. Introduction

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds related to your purchase and use of EtsyPro AI's services. By using our services, you agree to this Refund Policy.

2. Token System

EtsyPro AI operates on a token-based system:

  • Each token has a monetary value of $1 USD
  • Tokens are used to access various features of our service
  • Different service levels (Standard, Professional, Premium) require different token amounts

3. Refund Eligibility

3.1 Unused Tokens

Unused tokens may be eligible for a refund within 30 days of purchase under the following conditions:

  • Your account is in good standing
  • The tokens have not been applied to any service request
  • The refund request is made within 30 days of the original purchase

3.2 Used Tokens

Once tokens have been used to generate content (including titles, descriptions, tags, or images), they are not eligible for a refund, even if you are dissatisfied with the results.

3.3 Technical Issues

If our service fails to deliver any output due to technical issues on our end (server errors, system outages), the tokens used for that specific request will be automatically refunded to your account.

4. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • You changed your mind about using our service after content was generated
  • You are dissatisfied with the quality of the generated content
  • You violated our Terms of Service
  • Your Etsy account has issues unrelated to our service
  • You claim the content does not meet Etsy's requirements (we cannot guarantee Etsy approval)
  • More than 30 days have passed since the original purchase of unused tokens

5. Refund Process

To request a refund for eligible unused tokens:

  1. Log in to your EtsyPro AI account
  2. Navigate to the "Billing" or "Account" section
  3. Select "Request Refund" for the applicable token purchase
  4. Complete the refund request form, providing the reason for your request
  5. Submit your request

We will review refund requests within 5 business days. If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the purchase. Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider (typically 5-10 business days).

6. Promotional and Bonus Credits

Promotional tokens, bonus credits, or tokens provided as part of a special offer are not eligible for cash refunds under any circumstances. These tokens expire 12 months after issuance if unused.

7. Subscription Cancellations

While we primarily operate on a token-based system, if you have enrolled in any subscription offering:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No partial refunds are provided for unuse
